Terms & Conditions

Reservations:

Swiss Cottage Tioman takes reservations online through its website and through the booking agent booking.com

If you book through our website, we do not ask for deposits or credit card details, all that we ask is that guests inform us ahead of schedule if they would like to cancel their reservation.

We will be in contact with guests (that have made reservations) through the contact details they have provided, to confirm arrival time and ferry details.

Cancellation Policy

PLEASE READ THE FOLLOWING INFORMATION CAREFULLY. As we are a small B&B property, cancellations, changes and no-shows affect us greatly. We take great care to reserve your room and have it ready just for you. We understand there are unfortunate events which cause you to change or cancel your reservation. If you would like to cancel your reservation please let us know ahead of schedule.

Booking.com

Guests who have made reservations through booking.com and do not arrive for their scheduled reservation without notice ( NO SHOW ) will be charged the full amount of their reservation to the card on file.

Booking more then 2 rooms

If you would like to book more then 2 rooms we will ask you to pay a 50% deposit. The deposit is non refundable

Ferry Tickets

When a reservation is made we will ask the guest to send us their ferry ticket details. Guests must book the ferry in advance, the ferry has limited spaces especially on weekends and public holidays. Swiss Cottage Tioman is not responsible for booking ferry tickets on the guests behalf.

Breakfast

Swiss Cottage Tioman provides breakfast to guests who are staying at the resort. Guest can choose 1 meal per person from 4 options available. To see what we have available, click here.

Tea, coffee & drinking water

Swiss Cottage Tioman offers tea, coffee, hot water & drinking water free of charge to guests staying st Swiss Cottage Tioman. The service area is in the reception area, please help yourself.

Quiet Time

Swiss Cottage Tioman is committed to providing guests a peaceful and relaxing stay. To accommodate this, we ask that guests respect others by observing a quiet time between the hours of 9 pm and 8 am. Those staying with us who disturb other guests will be asked to leave and will not be provided a refund.

Events

If you have a small event (wedding, bridal shower, anniversary celebration, etc.), we’d love to talk with you about how we may accommodate your needs. Prices and availability will be determined at the time of reservations. If you have friends and family who are not staying with us during a regular stay, it is your responsibility to limit their visit time so as to not disturb our other guests.

Getting to Tioman

Swiss Cottage Tioman is located at Kampung Tekek, Pulau Tioman

Ferries

Ferries to Tioman leave from Mersing & Tanjung Gemok. There are currently 2 ferry operators bluewater.my & cataferry.com

Booking your ferry ticket

Ferry tickets can now be purchased online via the relevant companies mentioned above. Please purchase your ferry tickets in advance as the ferry can be fully booked especially on weekends and public holidays

Ferry Departure Time

The departure time for ferries varies, ferry timetables will be made available 1 month in advance. We will post the ferry timetable to our FB page for reference.

Getting to Swiss Cottage from the jetty in Kg Tekek

Swiss Cottage Tioman is located exactly 941m from the Bridge outside the Ferry terminal. Upon exiting the terminal, cross the bridge and turn right onto the road, walk until the road begins to incline, Swiss Cottage is on your right.

We can arrange for a taxi to collect you from the jetty in Kg Tekek. The cost will be RM5 per person. If you would like to take advantage of this service. Please send us the following details :

About Us

Swiss Cottage Tioman is a small resort located on Pulau Tioman. Founded in the 1980's when there was virtually no tourism on the island, Swiss Cottage has continued to provide accommodation to travelers wishing to get closer to Nature. All though the island has changed a lot since those early days Swiss Cottage still retains its Bohemian character.
